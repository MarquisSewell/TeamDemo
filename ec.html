  • Englewood Codes

    Englewood Codes is a ten week project in which teens from the neighborhood of Englewood are chosen. During the ten week period the students will learn HTML, CSS, Javascript, Bootstrap, and Github. Students will showcase their websites and recieve Raberry Pi's to keep their websites on at the end of the program.
